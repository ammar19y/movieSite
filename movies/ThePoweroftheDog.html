The Power of the Dog

2021 , Benedict Cumberbatch

2h 6m

Drama , Westrean

In the vast expanse of 1925 Montana, the Burbank brothers, wealthy ranchers, encounter Rose and her son Peter at the Red Mill restaurant. Phil, with his raw charm and brutal ways, leaves a trail of hurt and laughter, except for his brother George, who finds solace in Rose's company and eventually marries her. Phil's cruelty takes eerie forms, haunting Rose's vision and tormenting Peter. Yet, amidst his taunts, Phil inexplicably takes Peter under his wing, blurring the lines between softening and deeper menace, leaving all to wonder about his true intentions in this captivating tale of power, fragility, and tangled emotions on the frontier.
